Jeff Östberg

Jeff Östberg is a Swedish Illustrator currently living and working in Stockholm. Growing up in a small town in Northern Sweden, Jeff developed a love for drawing from a young age, influenced by the works of comic artists such as Hergé, Joost Swarte and Vittorio Giardino. He works with everything from editorial illustration to self-initiated projects. My pictures are often characterised by people that he sees in his everyday life, fluid shades and soft colours.
